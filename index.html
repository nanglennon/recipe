Pretzel Bites

with cheese

Ingredients:

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 can refrigerated biscuit dough
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup baking soda
  • 1 large egg
  • coarse salt
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 2 tbsp. flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar
  • Kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • cayenne pepper

Making the pretzels

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper and coat it with cooking spray.
  2. Quarter each biscuit and roll it into a ball. Carve an x on each ball using a knife
  3. In a medium saucepan, combine the water and baking soda - bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer. Add balls and cook for 30 seconds. (The dough will float to the top.)
  4. Transfer the dough balls to the baking sheet, making sure none are touching. Then brush beaten egg over each pretzel, and sprinkle with coarse salt.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until golden brown. Serve with cheese.

Making the dip

  1. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute.
  2. Stir in milk and bring to a simmer. When milk begins to thicken, stir in cheese
  3. Whisk constantly until sauce is smooth. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and cayenne. Serve with pretzels.
